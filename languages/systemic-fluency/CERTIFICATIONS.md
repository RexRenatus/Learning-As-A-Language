## üåê CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate)

### **Overview**
- **Full name**: Cisco Certified Network Associate
- **Exam code**: 200-301 CCNA
- **Duration**: 120 minutes
- **Questions**: 100-120 questions
- **Passing score**: ~825/1000 (scaled scoring, varies slightly)
- **Cost**: $300 USD
- **Validity**: 3 years (renew before expiration)

### **Prerequisites**
- None (open to anyone)
- Recommended: Basic computer and networking knowledge

### **Exam Topics & Weights**

1. **Network Fundamentals (20%)**
   - OSI and TCP/IP models
   - IPv4 and IPv6 addressing
   - Network topologies
   - Wireless principles

2. **Network Access (20%)**
   - VLANs and trunking
   - Spanning Tree Protocol
   - EtherChannel
   - Wireless LAN concepts

3. **IP Connectivity (25%)**
   - Routing fundamentals
   - Static and dynamic routing
   - OSPF, EIGRP
   - IPv6 routing

4. **IP Services (10%)**
   - NAT, DHCP, DNS, NTP
   - SNMP, Syslog
   - TFTP/FTP

5. **Security Fundamentals (15%)**
   - Security concepts
   - ACLs
   - Wireless security
   - VPNs

6. **Automation and Programmability (10%)**
   - Network automation tools
   - REST APIs
   - JSON, XML
   - Configuration management

### **Question Types**
- Multiple choice (single answer)
- Multiple choice (multiple answers)
- Drag-and-drop
- **Lab simulations** (configure actual Cisco equipment virtually)
- Testlets (scenario-based question groups)

### **Study Resources**
‚úÖ **Official Materials**:
- CCNA 200-301 Official Cert Guide Volume 1 & 2 (Wendell Odom)
- Cisco Learning Network
- Cisco Packet Tracer (free simulator)

‚úÖ **Free Resources**:
- Jeremy's IT Lab (YouTube) - Complete CCNA course
- David Bombal YouTube channel
- Cisco Skills For All (free courses)

‚úÖ **Practice Exams**:
- Boson ExSim (~$99, highly recommended)
- MeasureUp Practice Tests
- Cisco Official Practice Questions

### **Passing Strategy**
1. **Master subnetting** - appears throughout exam
2. **Practice labs extensively** - simulation questions are significant portion
3. **Understand routing protocols deeply** (OSPF, EIGRP)
4. **Memorize port numbers** and protocol details
5. **Take multiple practice exams** before attempting real exam
6. **Score 850+ on Boson** before scheduling real exam

### **After Passing**
- Certificate issued immediately
- Digital badge from Cisco
- Listed in Cisco Certification Tracker
- Valid for 3 years
- Update resume, LinkedIn

## ‚öñÔ∏è CISM (Certified Information Security Manager)

### **Overview**
- **Full name**: Certified Information Security Manager
- **Issuing body**: ISACA (Information Systems Audit and Control Association)
- **Exam code**: CISM
- **Duration**: 4 hours
- **Questions**: 150 multiple-choice questions
- **Passing score**: 450/800 (scaled scoring)
- **Cost**: $575 (ISACA member) / $760 (non-member)
- **Validity**: Must maintain with 20 CPE hours/year

### **Prerequisites for EXAM**
- None (anyone can take the exam)

### **Prerequisites for CERTIFICATION** ‚ö†Ô∏è
- **5 years** of information security work experience
- **3 years** must be in information security management
- Experience must be within a 10-year period
- Must submit application within 5 years of passing exam

**Experience Substitutions (maximum 2 years)**:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree ‚Üí up to 2 years
- CISA or CISSP certification ‚Üí up to 2 years
- **Remaining minimum**: 3 years actual security management work

### **Exam Domains & Weights**

1. **Information Security Governance (17%)**
   - Enterprise governance frameworks
   - Security strategy development
   - Organizational structures and roles
   - Regulatory and legal requirements
   - Security policies, standards, procedures

2. **Information Security Risk Management (20%)**
   - Risk identification and assessment
   - Risk treatment methodologies
   - Threat and vulnerability management
   - Risk monitoring and reporting
   - Business impact analysis

3. **Information Security Program Development and Management (33%)**
   - Security program framework
   - Security architecture and design
   - Information asset classification
   - Security control implementation
   - Awareness and training programs
   - Third-party risk management
   - Metrics and reporting

4. **Information Security Incident Management (30%)**
   - Incident response planning
   - Incident classification and escalation
   - Business continuity and disaster recovery
   - Forensics and evidence handling
   - Post-incident review

### **Question Format**
- All multiple choice (4 options, select best answer)
- Scenario-based questions testing management judgment
- Focus on "what should you do" vs "what can you do"
- Emphasizes management perspective, not technical implementation

### **Study Resources**
‚úÖ **Official Materials** (Required):
- CISM Review Manual (15th Edition) - ISACA (~$125-225)
- CISM QAE Database (practice questions) - ISACA (~$75-125)
- CISM Exam Content Outline (free from ISACA)

‚úÖ **Optional Resources**:
- CISM Review Questions, Answers & Explanations (supplemental Q&A book)
- LinkedIn Learning: CISM courses
- YouTube: CISM exam prep videos

‚úÖ **Recommended**:
- ISACA Membership ($135/year) - saves money on materials and exam

### **Passing Strategy**
1. **Study all 4 domains systematically** - don't skip any
2. **Focus on Domain 3** (33% of exam) - largest portion
3. **Think like a manager** - choose answers from leadership perspective
4. **Understand frameworks** - NIST, ISO 27001, COBIT (don't memorize details)
5. **Practice 1,000+ questions** before exam
6. **Score 75%+ on practice exams** before attempting real exam
7. **Read questions carefully** - often testing "best" answer, not just "correct" answer
