## üß† Recurring Mental Models & Patterns

These cross-cutting concepts appear across multiple disciplines:

### 1. Strategic Thinking
- **Appears in**: Game Theory, Military Strategy, IR, Intelligence, Economics
- **Core concept**: "Look forward and reason backward"
- **Transfer learning opportunity**: Master once in Game Theory, apply everywhere

### 2. Systems Thinking
- **Appears in**: Economics (macro), Sociology, Ecology, Cybersecurity
- **Core concept**: Feedback loops, emergence, complex adaptive systems
- **Transfer learning opportunity**: Unified mental model for all complex systems

### 3. Statistical Reasoning
- **Appears in**: Statistics, Data Science, Intelligence Analysis, Psychology
- **Core concept**: Uncertainty quantification, hypothesis testing, Bayesian updating
- **Transfer learning opportunity**: Master formal stats, apply to all data-driven fields

### 4. Evolutionary Thinking
- **Appears in**: Biology, Economics, Game Theory, Cultural Anthropology
- **Core concept**: Selection pressures, adaptation, fitness landscapes
- **Transfer learning opportunity**: Universal algorithm for understanding change

### 5. Psychological Biases
- **Appears in**: Psychology, Behavioral Economics, Intelligence Analysis, Marketing
- **Core concept**: System 1/2, heuristics and biases
- **Transfer learning opportunity**: Master once, apply to all human behavior

### 6. Network Analysis
- **Appears in**: Sociology, Computer Science, Intelligence (HUMINT), Epidemiology
- **Core concept**: Nodes, edges, centrality, clustering
- **Transfer learning opportunity**: Universal structure for relationships

### 7. Causal Reasoning
- **Appears in**: Statistics, Intelligence Analysis, Science, History
- **Core concept**: Correlation vs causation, causal inference, counterfactuals
- **Transfer learning opportunity**: Master logic of causation, apply everywhere

### 8. Framing & Communication
- **Appears in**: Rhetoric, Intelligence Writing, Politics, Marketing
- **Core concept**: How language shapes thought
- **Transfer learning opportunity**: Meta-skill for all communication
